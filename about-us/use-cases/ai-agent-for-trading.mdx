title: "AI agent for trading"
---

Beyond managing thousands of wallets, we've built AI agent for trading. Execute token swaps across multiple wallets with simple prompts:

- Buy random \$1 - \$100 of DEGEN token `0x4ed4E862860beD51a9570b96d89aF5E1B0Efefed`: The AI randomly allocates  \$1 - \$100 per wallet and executes swaps
- Buy a total of \$100 of FARTCOIN token `9BB6NFEcjBCtnNLFko2FqVQBq8HHM13kCyYcdQbgpump`: The AI distributes the order across wallets to total \$100
- Buy \$10 per wallet of FARTCOIN token `9BB6NFEcjBCtnNLFko2FqVQBq8HHM13kCyYcdQbgpump`: Each wallet purchases \$10 of FARTCOIN
- Sell 30% of FARTCOIN token `9BB6NFEcjBCtnNLFko2FqVQBq8HHM13kCyYcdQbgpump`: The AI agent sells 30% of FARTCOIN from each wallet
