title: "Manage thousands of multichain wallets"
---

Powerful wallet management lets you handle thousands of wallets across chains seamlessly.

You can track balances across multiple wallets for advanced trading strategies. For example, you can swap a token only from wallets that have more than $100 of SOL, or buy FARTCOIN token `9BB6NFEcjBCtnNLFko2FqVQBq8HHM13kCyYcdQbgpump` only if the wallet does not already have FARTCOIN... and countless custom workflows for efficient asset management across multiple wallets and chains.
