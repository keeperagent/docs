---
title: "Monitor liquidity position"
---

Build workflows to manage LP positions on Meteora, Raydium, Uniswap, and Pancakeswap -applicable in many use cases, such as:

- Monitor token price and market cap from Dexscreener to close positions
- Track position fees, if fees exceed \$100, claim, swap, and compound back into the position
- Track position fees, if fees exceed \$100, claim and transfer the claimed tokens to another wallet
